How I work

The habits behind the work: how I approach systems, constraints, and artifacts.

Operating style

I’m drawn to problems where the elegant answer has to survive reality: noisy data, flaky hardware, changing requirements, team handoffs, and deadlines.

  • Make the thing work end-to-end before getting precious about the architecture.
  • Respect the physics: sensors, latency, calibration, and manufacturing always matter.
  • Prefer artifacts, demos, and measured behavior over vague capability claims.
